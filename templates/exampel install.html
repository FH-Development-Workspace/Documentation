SafeGas V2 Installation

SafeGas, the ro-industrys most advanced and secure gas system, is now available for installation. This guide will walk you through the steps to install SafeGas V2 on your system. Please ensure that you have the necessary permissions and access rights before proceeding with the installation.

1

Place the file in Workspace

After downloading the product file, drag and drop into Workspace under your game.
2

Ensure HTTP requests/API services are enabled

Check to make sure your security settings are correct. You can do this by heading to Home in Roblox Studio, then Security Settings.
