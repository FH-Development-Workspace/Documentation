SafeGas V2 Configuration

SafeGas, the ro-industrys most advanced and secure gas system, is now available for configuration. This guide will walk you through the steps to configure SafeGas V2 on your system. Please ensure that you have the necessary permissions and access rights before proceeding with the configuration.

1

Add Whitelists

Once your product is in game, open the folders and find the Whitelist Module. Replace each rank with your Roblox Group Ranks and Roblox Group ID in the module. This will ensure that only the ranks you have set can use gas panels.
2

Move the Panel

Put the GasPanel somewhere in the room in which Staff can access it.
3

Duplicate Bunsen Burners

Move and duplicate the bunsen burners to wherever around the area. Remember that the bunsens in each folder are specific to that zone. The bunsens in Zone 1 can't be controlled by the panel in Zone 2. Make sure to duplicate the burners in each zone to ensure that they are all functional.
4

Finish!

Test out your new gas system - product does not work in Studio.
ⓘ

Panel Support:
TURNING ON:
-> Click the Brown button
-> Press inwards the top black button
(Wait for the red light on the display) -> Turn on a bunsen burner
TURNING OFF:
-> Press the second black button inwards
(Wait for red light to go off and LCD to change)
(All bunsens should turn off)
